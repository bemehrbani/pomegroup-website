At **Pomegroup Studio**, we do not believe in the traditional agency model. We don't want to build software for you, hand over the keys, and walk away. Instead, we act as your **second co-founder**—fully aligned on risk, equity, and execution. 

We turn domain expertise into market-ready digital products. If you are an industry specialist who knows a problem inside out but lacks the technical execution, we build it with you.

---

## Why Venture Studios Beat Agencies

Traditional agencies charge massive hourly rates, have no skin in the game, and are incentivized to prolong development timelines. Freelancers are hard to manage and rarely scale.

Here is how the venture studio model aligns interests:

1. **Shared Risk (Co-Founder Model)**: We invest our team's engineering, design, and product strategy resources into the venture in exchange for equity. 
2. **Speed to Market**: We don't spend months negotiating contracts. We build lean, high-value Minimum Viable Tests (MVTs) and launch them immediately.
3. **Continuous Execution**: We support the venture through fundraising, initial client onboarding, and technical scaling.

## The Co-Founder Framework

We follow a simple 4-step framework to launch new ventures:

### 1. Identify & Screen
We speak with domain experts to identify high-pain bottlenecks in traditional industries. We screen these using our ICE (Impact, Confidence, Ease) framework.

### 2. MVT (Minimum Viable Test)
Within weeks, we deploy a functional prototype. For example, before building a complete SaaS platform, we release a high-value checklist or interactive calculator to verify buyer intent.

### 3. Co-Found & Build
If the MVT shows strong interest (such as client sign-ups or inbound demo requests), we formally co-found the business, establish the entity, and build out the full-scale application.

### 4. Scale & Transition
We help secure early customer wins and seed financing, and eventually transition the product to an in-house engineering team once mature.
